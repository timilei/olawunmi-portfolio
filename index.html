A Little About My Journey

From configuring routers to hunting for anomalies in traffic captures — here's what drives me.

Why Cybersecurity?

My path into cybersecurity started with a simple curiosity: how do systems actually break, and how do we stop it? That question took me from an Ordinary National Diploma (OND) in Networking and System Security into full-time undergraduate study in Cybersecurity, where I now focus on network defense, system hardening, and threat analysis.

What I Do

I build and break lab networks to understand attacker behaviour, analyze packet captures to spot intrusions, and write Python scripts to automate the boring parts of security monitoring. Every project on this site is a step toward becoming a security professional organisations can trust with their infrastructure.
